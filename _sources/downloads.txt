Data from the JoCN Methods paper:
--------------------------------

Here are the csv matrices for the 5 **EndGraphs** discussed in the paper. These Endgraphs have as input the entire CoCoMac corpus. Subcortical nodes have been removed. Please check out the paper for details.

1. Download PHT00 matrices:  :download:`click here for ORT<downloads_copy\\pht00_endg_ort.csv>` and :download:`click here for mORT<downloads_copy\\pht00_endg_mort.csv>`.

2. Download R00 matrices:  :download:`click here for ORT<downloads_copy\\r00_endg_ort.csv>` and :download:`click here for mORT<downloads_copy\\r00_endg_mort.csv>`.

3. Download FV91 matrices:  :download:`click here for ORT<downloads_copy\\fv91_endg_ort.csv>` and :download:`click here for mORT<downloads_copy\\fv91_endg_mort.csv>`.

4. Download B05 matrices:  :download:`click here for ORT<downloads_copy\\B05_endg_ort.csv>` and :download:`click here for mORT<downloads_copy\\B05_endg_mort.csv>`.

5. Download BB47 matrices:  :download:`click here for ORT<downloads_copy\\BB47_endg_ort.csv>` and :download:`click here for mORT<downloads_copy\\BB47_endg_mort.csv>`.
